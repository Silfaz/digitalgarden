### Recommended fabrics
-   Weight: light to medium-heavy (180 to 340 gsm or 4-8 oz.)
-   Fabric: woven (denim, serge, velvet, flannel, wool...)
-   Stretch factor*: 10-15 % with 1 to 2 % max elastane
