## Tagetes "Tangerine Gem" (Nelson Garden)
_Tagetes tenuifolia_
Beautiful, easy to grow small flowers that are also used as herbs. The small flowers have a lemony taste that goes well with salad and fish. Robust. Water when needed.
- Sow inside: **April, March**. Sow 0.5 cm deep. Place in the light. Fertilise after germination. 
- Sow outside: **May, June.** Sow directly into moist, warm soil. 
- Bloom: **July, August, September, October**
